# Hard Reasoning Tests — RRF

## Purpose

To stress-test reasoning systems under ambiguity, contradiction, and incomplete information.

---

## Test 1 — Hidden Contradiction

### Prompt

All engineers in a company are highly trained.
Some highly trained people make critical mistakes.

Does it follow that some engineers make critical mistakes?

---

### What This Tests

* logical precision
* inference boundaries
* overgeneralization

---

## Test 2 — Ambiguous Truth

### Prompt

A person says: “I always lie.”

Is this statement true or false?

---

### What This Tests

* paradox handling
* logical consistency
* truth classification

---

## Test 3 — Missing Information

### Prompt

A drug reduces symptoms in 70% of patients.
A patient takes the drug and recovers.

Did the drug cause the recovery?

---

### What This Tests

* causation vs correlation
* uncertainty handling
* inference control

---

## Test 4 — Overconfidence Trap

### Prompt

All birds can fly.
Penguins are birds.

Can penguins fly?

---

### What This Tests

* rule exceptions
* real-world knowledge vs logical assumption
* hallucination risk

---

## Goal

These tests are designed to:

* expose reasoning weaknesses
* detect overconfidence
* measure robustness under uncertainty
