# Real Model Evaluation — RRF

## Purpose

To evaluate real AI model outputs using the Ralone Recursive Framework (RRF).

---

## Test Used

Real Test 2 — Context & Truth Evaluation

---

## Model: ChatGPT

### Output

Water does not always boil at exactly 100°C.
At higher altitudes, atmospheric pressure is lower, which lowers the boiling point of water.

---

### Evaluation

#### RTS — Truth Scale

* factually correct
* correctly applies condition (pressure affects boiling point)
* no hallucination

Score: 100

---

#### RLGE — Logic Grounding Engine

* valid reasoning chain
* correct cause-effect relationship (altitude → pressure → boiling point)
* no contradiction

Score: 100

---

#### RVE — Verification Engine

* consistent with known physics
* fully verifiable
* no unsupported claims

Score: 100

---

## Composite Score

RRF Score = (100 + 100 + 100) / 3 = 100

---

## Result

This model demonstrates strong reasoning, correct contextual awareness, and full verifiability.
